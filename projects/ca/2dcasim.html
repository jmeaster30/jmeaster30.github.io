    Compilers/Interpreters Cellular Automata Games
This program allows you to simulate any 2d cellular automata that you can think of. The freedom of this simulator comes from the ability to embed a lua script with the instructions for your cellular automaton. I wrote a script for Conway's Game of Life, Brian's Brain, and the Wire World cellular automata and they are located in the "automata" folder.
